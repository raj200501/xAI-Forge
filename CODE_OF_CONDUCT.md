# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on constructive feedback.
- Respect boundaries and privacy.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Deliberate disruption of community spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take action to address unacceptable behavior, including
content removal or banning participants.

## Reporting

Report concerns to `security@xaiforge.local`.
